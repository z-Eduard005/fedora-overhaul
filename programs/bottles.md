# Bottles

*The best way to launch windows apps or games on linux*

## Post-install
1. Create your first bottle: + -> choose -> if possible, choose latest ge-proton
2. Add your windows programs/games:
  2.1. choose your bottle -> Browse C:/ drive -> users/steamuser/Downloads/ -> Add here your installer
  2.2. choose your bottle -> Run Executable... -> choose your installer
